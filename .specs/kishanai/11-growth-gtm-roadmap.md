# 11 - Growth and Go-to-Market Roadmap: KisanAI

## Document Purpose
Define the 90-day pilot plan, GTM channels, pricing strategy, growth loops, and expansion roadmap for KisanAI. This is the operational playbook for acquiring, activating, and retaining farmers.

---

## 1. 90-Day Pilot Plan

### 1.1 Pilot Overview

| Dimension | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| Duration | 90 days (3 phases of 30 days each) |
| Target farmers | 500-1,000 |
| Geography | 1 state, 1 agro-climatic belt |
| Crop focus | 1-2 crops (e.g., tomato + onion) |
| Language | 1 regional language + Hindi |
| Distribution | B2B2C through 2-3 FPOs/NGOs |
| Success metrics | Activation, retention, quality, CSAT |

### 1.2 Phase 1: Validation (Days 1-30)

**Objective**: Validate product-market fit with early users

| Week | Activities | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Week 1 | Finalize FPO/NGO partnerships | Signed MOUs |
| Week 1 | Set up pilot infrastructure | WhatsApp bot, PWA, admin dashboard |
| Week 2 | Onboard first 100 farmers (manual) | 100 active users |
| Week 2 | Conduct 20 farmer interviews | Interview insights document |
| Week 3 | Iterate based on feedback | Updated product features |
| Week 4 | Expand to 250 farmers | 250 active users |

**Key Metrics**:
- Activation rate: > 50% (first query within 24h)
- CSAT: > 60% positive
- Query completion rate: > 70%

**Exit Criteria**:
- 100+ farmers have used KisanAI at least once
- 50+ farmers have used it more than 3 times
- CSAT > 60%
- No critical safety incidents

### 1.3 Phase 2: Refinement (Days 31-60)

**Objective**: Improve quality, expand features, grow user base

| Week | Activities | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Week 5 | Analyze Phase 1 data | Quality report |
| Week 5 | Improve disease model accuracy | Updated model |
| Week 6 | Add mandi price feature | Price integration |
| Week 6 | Add scheme explainer | Scheme database |
| Week 7 | Expand to 500 farmers | 500 active users |
| Week 8 | Run A/B tests on prompts | Optimized prompts |

**Key Metrics**:
- Day-7 retention: > 25%
- Grounded answer rate: > 75%
- Disease diagnosis accuracy: > 70% top-1
- Active users: 500+

**Exit Criteria**:
- Day-7 retention > 25%
- Grounded answer rate > 75%
- 500+ active users
- Positive feedback rate > 65%

### 1.4 Phase 3: Scale (Days 61-90)

**Objective**: Prove scalability and prepare for expansion

| Week | Activities | Deliverables |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Week 9 | Optimize for cost | Cost reduction plan |
| Week 10 | Add admin dashboard features | FPO analytics |
| Week 11 | Expand to 1,000 farmers | 1,000 active users |
| Week 12 | Prepare expansion plan | Second-cluster proposal |
| Week 13 | Pilot review and deck update | Investor deck with data |

**Key Metrics**:
- Day-30 retention: > 15%
- Active users: 1,000+
- Gross margin: > 70%
- NPS: > 30

**Exit Criteria**:
- Day-30 retention > 15%
- 1,000+ active users
- Positive unit economics
- Ready for Series A conversations

### 2.2 FPO Partnership Playbook

**Step 1: Identify Target FPOs**
- 500+ member farmers
- Active management
- Growing commercial crops
- Technology-friendly leadership

**Step 2: Initial Outreach**
- Contact FPO chairman/manager
- Offer free 30-day pilot for 50 farmers
- Provide WhatsApp demo
- Share ROI projections

**Step 3: Onboarding**
- 2-hour orientation for FPO staff
- Create co-branded WhatsApp group
- Set up admin dashboard access
- Provide marketing materials

**Step 4: Launch**
- Announce in FPO WhatsApp group
- Send welcome message to all members
- Offer live demo session
- Provide QR code for easy access

**Step 5: Support**
- Weekly check-in calls
- Monthly performance review
- Quarterly business review
- Dedicated support WhatsApp number

### 2.3 Retailer Partnership Playbook

**Step 1: Identify Target Retailers**
- High foot traffic location
- Serves 200+ farmers
- Technology-friendly owner
- Sells crop protection products

**Step 2: Value Proposition**
- "Help your customers with better advice"
- "Increase customer trust and loyalty"
- "Free tool -- no cost to you"

**Step 3: Onboarding**
- 30-minute orientation session
- Provide QR code poster for shop
- Show how to use KisanAI for diagnosis
- Add to retailer WhatsApp group

**Step 4: Activation**
- Retailer uses KisanAI for walk-in diagnoses
- Farmers discover KisanAI through QR code
- Retailer shares answer cards with customers

### 2.4 WhatsApp Group Strategy

**Growth Loop**:
```
Farmer asks question in KisanAI
    |
    v

    |
    v

    |

    |
    v
Cycle repeats
```

**Content Strategy for Groups**:
- Daily weather advisory (morning)
- Weekly price summary (Monday)
- Seasonal crop tips

## 3. Pricing Strategy

### 3.1 Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Features | Target |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| **Free** | Rs 0 | 10 queries/day, basic features | Individual farmers |
| **Basic** | Rs 49/month or Rs 399/year | Unlimited queries, priority response, disease diagnosis | Active farmers |
| **FPO Basic** | Rs 2,000/month | 200 members, basic analytics, advisory broadcasts | Small FPOs |
| **FPO Premium** | Rs 5,000/month | 500 members, advanced analytics, co-branding | Medium FPOs |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | API access, custom features, dedicated support | Large organizations |

### 3.2 Pricing Rationale

**Why Rs 49/month for B2C**:
- Less than one bad spray decision (Rs 200-500 waste)
- Less than one mandi trip cost (Rs 100-300)
- Comparable to mobile recharge (Rs 199-599/month)
- Affordable for 2+ acre farmers

**Why Rs 2,000/month for FPO Basic**:
- Rs 10/farmer/month for 200 farmers
- Less than cost of one field visit by staff
- Scalable advisory without hiring

### 3.3 Free Trial Strategy

| Trial Type | Duration | Features | Conversion Target |
|-----------|----------|----------|-------------------|
| Farmer free trial | 30 days | All features | 15-20% to paid |
| FPO pilot | 30 days | 50 farmers, basic features | 30-40% to paid |
| NGO pilot | 90 days | 100 farmers, full features | 50%+ to paid |

### 3.4 Payment Methods

| Method | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| UPI | Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm |
| Bank transfer | Direct bank transfer |
| Cash (via retailer) | Retailer collects and credits |
| FPO billing | Invoice to FPO, bank transfer |

## 7. Metrics Dashboard

### 7.1 North Star Metric
**Weekly Active Query Users (WAQU)**: Unique users who ask at least 1 query per week

### 7.2 Acquisition Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target (Month 3) |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| New users | Users who complete first query | 1,000 |
| Cost per acquisition (CPA) | Total spend / new users | < Rs 100 |
| Partner-to-user conversion | Users per FPO/NGO | 50-100 per partner |
| Channel distribution | % users by channel | WhatsApp 60%, PWA 30%, Telegram 10% |

### 7.3 Activation Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| First query rate | % new users who ask first query | > 70% |
| Time to first query | Median time from signup to first query | < 5 minutes |
| First image upload | % users who upload first image | > 30% |
| Onboarding completion | % users who complete language/location setup | > 50% |

### 7.4 Retention Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Day-1 retention | % users who return next day | > 50% |
| Day-7 retention | % users who return within 7 days | > 30% |
| Day-30 retention | % users who return within 30 days | > 15% |

### 7.5 Quality Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Grounded answer rate | % answers with source citation | > 80% |
| Positive feedback rate | % positive thumbs up | > 70% |

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|

## 10. Risk Mitigation

### 10.1 GTM Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Low farmer adoption | Medium | High | Manual onboarding, FPO support, WhatsApp distribution |
| Poor answer quality | Medium | High | Curated knowledge base, human review, abstention |
| FPO partner churn | Low | Medium | Multiple partners, clear value demonstration |
| Competitor entry | Medium | Medium | Speed of execution, local relationships |
| Regulatory changes | Low | Medium | Compliance-first approach, legal monitoring |

### 10.2 Contingency Plans

| Scenario | Trigger | Response |
|----------|---------|----------|
| Low adoption | < 100 users after 30 days | Pivot to different channel or geography |
| Poor retention | Day-7 < 15% | Deep user research, product iteration |
| Quality issues | > 10% negative feedback | Increase human review, narrow scope |
| Cost overrun | AI costs > budget | Switch to cheaper models, reduce features |
| Partner loss | Key FPO drops out | Pre-identify backup partners |
